Lowest NPS Scores Analysis

NPS 2.0 (PROD):

"There was far too much unstructured time and "fluff" in this program. We were often left on our own to "work on a project," which felt unrealistic given that we were expected to learn new tools and implement a solution by the end of day three. After the first day, there was minimal hands‑on use of the technology.
